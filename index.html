DATA VISUALIZATION

The first project we completed for this class was creating a Data Visualization from data that we had collected from our own lives. I wanted to pick something that I knew I could get a lot of data points for, that would also be easy for me to remember to record. That's why I decided to do my project on the music I listen to.

DATA COLLECTION

In order to collect the data to create an accurate and meaningful project I had to record every song that I listened to for a week. But I did not just want the songs I listened to because that does not give much depth to the project, and there would not be much for me to learn about myself. So I also collected information about myself When I was listening to the music: how was I feeling? what was I doing? how strong the emotion I was feeling was? All of these factors allowed me to learn a lot more about myself, and about music and how it affects me.

This chart allowed me to highlight the correlation between the mood I'm in and what I'm doing to the kind of music I listen to. For example, I listen to more pop and rap when I'm happy or excited, but I listen to more country and rock when I'm more relaxed or focused. My marks and attributes amplify these patterns by showing clear differences between the genres I listen to. The 'Pop' genre has more long lines showing I'm happy or excited, whereas the 'Country' section has a lot more short lines showing I'm relaxed and content. Just the size of these two sections being so different shows how much my mood correlates to the music I listen to.
